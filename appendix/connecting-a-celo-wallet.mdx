1. Click "Connect Wallet" on Spirals dApp
2. Choose "Other Wallets"
3. Click "Copy to clipboard"
4. Navigate to your Celo Wallet in a separate browser
5. Click "More" then "Connect"
6. Paste from your clipboard then click "Connect" then "Approve"
7. Return to the Spirals dApp - in the top right corner it should now show your
   wallet address

Now you're all ready to start staking for climate impact! Great work.
